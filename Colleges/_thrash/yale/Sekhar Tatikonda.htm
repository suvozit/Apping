INTERESTS
Communication theory, information theory, sensor networks, stochastic control, distributed estimation and control, statistical machine learning and inference.
 

REPRESENTATIVE PUBLICATIONS

  • Feedback Capacity of Finite-State Machine Channels, S. Yang, A. Kavcic, Sekhar Tatikonda, 2005, IEEE Transactions on Automatic Control, 51, Issue 3, 799-810.
  • Control Under Communication Constraints, Sekhar Tatikonda, S. Mitter, 2004, IEEE Transactions on Automatic Control, 49, Issue 7, 1056-1068.
  • Control Over Noisy Channels, Sekhar Tatikonda, S. Mitter, 2004, IEEE Transactions on Automatic Control, 49, 1196-1201.
  • Stochastic Linear Control Over a Communication Channel, Sekhar Tatikonda, A. Sahai, S. Mitter, 2004, IEEE Transactions on Automatic Control, 49, Issue 9, 1549-1561.
  • Markov Control Problems Under Communication Constraints, Sekhar Tatikonda, S. Mitter, V. Borkar, 2001, Communications in Information and Systems (CIS), 1, 15-32.
  • Optimal Sequential Vector Quantization of Markov Sources, Sekhar Tatikonda, S. Mitter, V. Borkar, 2001, SIAM Journal on Control and Optimization (SICON), 40, 135-148.
