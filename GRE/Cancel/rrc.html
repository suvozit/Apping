Regional Registration Centers for GRE® Computer-based Tests in International Locations

The Regional Registration Centers (RRCs) and the countries associated with each region are listed below. E-mail addresses are for informational requests only.

This list contains the computer-based testing centers currently available. If there is no test center in your country, the computer-based test may not be available in your area.

To see the paper-based test centers in your area, see the test center list for the revised General Test.

Note: When calling an RRC that is located outside the country from which you are calling, you must dial the international access number.
