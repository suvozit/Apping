Getting Your Scores

If you need your scores reported prior to November 2011, you should take the current GRE® General Test before August 1, 2011.

Detailed Score Reporting Schedule

If you take the computer-based GRE® revised General Test between August and November, score reports will be issued according to the schedule below.

Computer-based revised General Test Dates Approximate Score Report Mailing Dates and View Scores Online Dates
August 1, 2011 – September 8, 2011 November 8, 2011
September 9, 2011 – October 2, 2011 November 10, 2011
October 3, 2011 – October 15, 2011 November 17, 2011
October 16, 2011 – November 18, 2011 December 1, 2011
November 19, 2011 – November 28, 2011 December 8, 2011
November 29, 2011 or later 10 – 15 days after the test date

Score reporting for the computer-based test will return to the regular reporting period of 10 to 15 days for test administrations on and after November 29, 2011. The paper-based test will continue to be sent within six weeks after you take the test. Scores for both tests can also be viewed online or received via mail or by phone.

View Scores Online

You can view your scores online free of charge. You will need to create or have a My GRE Account to use this service.

  • For computer-based revised General Test takers who take the test in December 2011 or later, the View Scores Online service is available approximately 15 days after your test date. If you take the test between August 2011 and November 2011, please review the detailed score reporting schedule above.
  • For paper-based revised General Test takers, the View Scores Online service is available approximately six weeks after your test date. See "Approximate Score Report Mailing Dates" for the dates when scores will be available online.

By Mail

You will receive an official score report from ETS by mail. Allow sufficient time for mail delivery from Princeton, New Jersey, USA. See sample score report (PDF).

Computer-based GRE revised General Test

Beginning in December 2011, official Verbal, Quantitative and Analytical Writing scores will be sent to you and the score recipients you designated within 10 to 15 days. For testing between August and November 2011, see the detailed score reporting schedule above to see the date your scores will be mailed.

Paper-based GRE revised General Test

Official scores will be sent to you and the score recipients you designated within six weeks after you take the test.

Paper-based GRE revised General Test Dates Approximate Score Report Mailing Dates View Scores Online
October 22, 2011 December 2, 2011
November 19, 2011 December 30, 2011
February 11, 2012 March 23, 2012

Scores By Phone

Fee: US$12

1-609-771-7290 or 1-888-473-7267 (toll free for test takers in the United States, American Samoa, Guam, Puerto Rico, U.S. Virgin Islands and Canada)

1-888-473-8333 (TTY)

Call 6 a.m. to 10 p.m. Eastern Time (New York), seven days a week.

Use these services only if you call from a touch-tone phone, pay by credit/debit card (American Express®, Discover®, JCB®, MasterCard® or VISA®) and have tested in the last five years. Wait for confirmation before hanging up. Once information is entered, changes cannot be made.

Have the following information ready:

  • registration number, Social Security number or confirmation number
  • test date
  • date of birth
  • institution code(s)
  • department code(s)

Occasionally delays in processing may occur. Some scores may not be available on the dates specified. Please call again.

Paper-based GRE revised General Test

Score reports from paper-based tests will be mailed approximately six weeks after the test date. To get scores sooner, call on or after the dates below. Scores earned between July 2006 and June 2011 are available at any time.

GRE revised General Test Date Call On or After
October 22, 2011 November 21, 2011
November 19, 2011 December 19, 2011
February 11, 2012 March 12, 2012

Computer-based GRE revised General Test

Starting in December 2011, score reports from computer-based tests will be mailed within 10 to 15 days after the test date. If you test between August 2011 and November 2011, review the detailed score reporting schedule above to see when your scores will be available. These scores will also be available by phone for a fee of US$12. For more information about when computer-based test scores will be available, see the detailed score reporting schedule above.

Additional Score Reports (ASRs) by Phone

Fee: US$23 per score recipient for up to eight recipients

Score reports will be mailed to you and to your designated institutions approximately five business days after your call. Scores must be initially reported before you can use this service. Institutions that receive scores on CD are sent reports approximately twice a month. Requests for "Test Taker Copies Only" or changes in your mailing address cannot be processed through this service.

For more information, see Additional Score Reports.
